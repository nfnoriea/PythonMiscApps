DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
This project involves the analysis of our UCR and non-UCR collaborator's deidentified whole blood and plasma samples from healthy humans. We will evaluate levels of lipids in whole blood and plasma from subjects with the goal of observing fluctuations depending upon feeding status. In addition, we will use non-replicating viral vectors, injected into the upper small intestine and brain of mice, to identify gut-brian physiology. Virus will induce expression of proteins that can be activated and in turn, activate specific neural pathways.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
We aim to evaluate fluctuations in circulating lipids as they relate to feeding status (e.g.,hungry/satiated). The use of viral vectors will allow for the examination of gut-brain feeding circuits and cross-talk between the gut and brain.
