DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
The long-term goal of the PI's project is to understand how plants respond to changes in their light environment. In particular, the project utilizes Arabidopsis thaliana as the genetic model and a combination of molecular genetics, biochemical, and genomic approaches to investigate the signaling mechanism by which the red/far-red photoreceptors, the phytochromes, control plant growth and development.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
1. Construction of recombinant DNA molecules to clone or subclone genes and genomic DNAs in E. coli and yeast. 2. Generation of transgenic Arabidopsis lines expressing tagged proteins to study the function and subcellular localization of light signaling molecules in plants.
