DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
We study plant insect and plant nematodes interactions. The aim is to identify plant genes regulating immunity against root-knot nematode or aphid pests. We also study the role of aphid saliva, secreted inside the plant host during feeding, in modulating plant immunity and metabolism. Therefore, we use tools that alter either plant genes or pest genes to assess their roles in this interaction.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
The objectives of our work are to: 1) identify plant genes that regulate immunity against pests and pathogens; 2) identify the metabolic pathways affecting the aphids due to Mi-1-resistance; 3) identify aphid effectors that modulate plant immunity; 4) identify plant genes that regulate immunity against pests and pathogens.
