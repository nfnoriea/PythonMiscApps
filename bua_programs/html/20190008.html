DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
-Biological detection and characterization of citrus pathogens by inoculation of selected plant indicators -Molecular characterization and detection of viral, bacterial, bacterial like, and fungal pathogens by nucleic acid extraction in vitro amplification, cloning, sequencing and in silico analysis -Serological characterization and production of specific antibodies -Molecular and Culture-Based assessment and characterization of microbiome associated with plants
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
Combination of the above technologies for the development of diagnostic protocols for use in the UCR California Citrus Clonal Protection Program and the citrus industry of the State as well as the Citrus Centers of the National Clean Plant Network.
