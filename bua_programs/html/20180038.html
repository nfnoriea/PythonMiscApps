DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
The proper folding of proteins is important to maintain health and avoid disease. We are developing chemical techniques that characterize protein folding, which we will then test in human cell culture.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
Human cell culture allows us to probe processes that occur in human cells. These processes cannot be reconstituted outside of cells. Many of the tools that we are developing rely on the intracellular production of bioengineered proteins. To introduce these artificial genes, we must construct recombinant DNA plasmids that encode these genes. To construct and produce recombinant DNA plasmids, we must grow them in laboratory strains of E. coli.
