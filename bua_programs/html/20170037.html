DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
The plant transformation research Center - PTRC is a state-of-the-art laboratory with the expertise to deliver genes into different target crops (alfalfa, arabidopsis, canola, citrus, potato, tomato, tobacco, and rice). Transgenic plants are routinely generated via Agrobacterium-mediated transformation or particle bombardment protocols. Preliminary screens are conducted to verify transformation events by monitoring the expression and/or integration of the selectable marker genes.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
- Provide the clientele with either the primary transformants or seeds derived from the primary transformants
