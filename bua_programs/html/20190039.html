DESCRIPTION OF PROJECT
Provide a brief, non-technical description and purpose of the project.
We strive to understand the molecular mechanisms underlying cell fate specification in plant development. We study how small RNAs are synthesized and degraded in the cell, how they move between cells, and how they function in gene regulation and in development. We investigate the mechanisms of RNA modifications. We study Arabidopsis, rice, tobacco, soybean, and maize plants. In collaboration with others, we are also studying how plants respond to pathogens but we do not directly work with the pathogens in our laboratory.
What are the specific objectives of the work with biohazardous materials?
We clone genes from the organisms, manipulate the genes such that we can study their function either in the test tube or in plants ( by producing transgenic plants). The gene manipulation process also involves the uses of bacteria such as E. coli and Agrobacteria. We also use viral vectors to perform virus-induced gene silencing in plants.
